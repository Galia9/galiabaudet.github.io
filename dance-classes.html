DANCE CLASSES

I have been a trainer for adults and a dancer for over 10 years. I offer dance workshops specially designed for adults who wish to start dancing.

Sensual Healing

Reconnect to your sensuality

Sensual Healing is dedicated to women who want to celebrate their femininity through sensual movements. This class is designed to boost confidence and self-esteem in a caring and supportive environment.

Harmony in Motion

Reconnect to your body

Harmony in Motion is dedicated to men & women who want to (re)connect with their bodies through the magic of contemporary dance. This class is an exploration of one's own rhythm and an affirmation of the freedom of movement.
