DANCE CLASSES

I have been a trainer for adults and a dancer for over 10 years. I offer dance workshops specially designed for adults who wish to start dancing.

Sensual Healing

Reconnect to your sensuality

With Sensual Healing, let’s celebrate our femininity through slow and sensual movements. This class is designed to boost confidence and self-esteem in a caring and supportive environment, where every moment becomes a celebration of oneself.

"Thank you Galia, without whom none of this would have been possible. Thank you for helping me reveal this woman within me."

Harmony in Motion

Reconnect to your body

Harmony in Motion is dedicated to those who want to connect or reconnect with their bodies through the magic of contemporary dance. This class is an exploration of one's own rhythm and an affirmation of the freedom of movement.

"Oh Galia, thank you for such a brilliant class! I loved how you tailored it to suit everyone, and your explanations were fantastic. You’re incredibly talented!"

How are my classes designed?

My dance classes are specifically designed for adults, by adults. I aim to create an environment where complete beginners can learn in a way that suits their needs, feeling supported and encouraged.
